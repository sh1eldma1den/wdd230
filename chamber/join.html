Experience the support of a community dedicated to your success.

Training | Education | Mentorship | Networking | Advertising | Deals

Training
We offer training for both you and your staff in multiple areas. Whether it's learning how to put together benefit packages for your employees or safety training for your employees, we are here to help you succeed.

Education
Want to know the tax implications of expansion? Regulations that apply to your business or location? Safety or compliance concerns? We offer education on a number of topics.

Mentorship
Starting a new venture? Our experienced members volunteer in a mentoring program aimed at helping our small, new business owners through the first year.

Networking
Attend our many networking events and join in the collaboration efforts in the Aiken community. Your business will thrive with the support of our vibrant and helpful network of professionals.

Advertising
Advertise with us! Enjoy three times as much exposure as a Chamber member listed in our directory. Customers are 80% more likely to buy from a Chamber member.

Deals
Enjoy members-only discounts with our city partners. Many within our network of qualified professionals offer discounts to fellow members.
