Lesson 2: Design Principles

Visual Heirarchy

Etsy

Etsy is a wonderful example of using the design principle of visual heirarchy. The eye flows easily through the suggested browsing categories and on to the suggested items for sale. These circular, picture categories welcome the viewer directly into the indulgent experience of visual browsing that etsy is famous for.

White Space and Clean Design

GameStop

Gamestop's website is very clean. White space surrounds everything, drawing the eye immediately to the images of products. The clean white space makes each photo stand out it stark contrast, and makes it quick and easy for the viewer to find what they are seeking.

Contrast

Netflix

Netflix's website uses contrast to immediately immerse the viewer in a media streaming experience. With a darkened backgound and white text, it almost feels as if you are at a darkened theater. The contrast of the white search bar reminds the viewer that the streaming possibilities are endless, and invites them to begin their experience immediately.
