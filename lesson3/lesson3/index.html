Site Description and Purpose

The client requested a responsive website of consistent design ideal for viewing on all devices of varying screen widths. The site displays for users the current weather conditions, weather forecast, news and events in and around the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven in Southeastern Idaho. The site will include the following pages: Home, Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, Storm Center, and Gallery. The home page displays current weather conditions with corresponding weather images and information sections for the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Town pages contain news, events, other community information in each town, as well as user-submitted images of weather and occasional contests. Page footers contain contact information, copyright, author's name, terms of use link, and current date. No login will be necessary, but there will be an input section for users to make suggestions, offer feedback, or respond to site weather photo contests.
