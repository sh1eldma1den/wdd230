    Fish Haven Canyon Road and Hogs Back Trail to Close for Repairs

    The popular Fish Haven Canyon Road (NFSR 413) and Hogs Back Trail adjacent to Bear Lake, which accesses portions of Forest in the Montpelier Ranger District, will close for repairs beginning March 1. The road and trail will close while heavy equipment is on the road, we ask you to be respectful of the staged equipment.

    Road Closed | March 1 - April 7
    Road Open | April 7 - April 28
    Road Closed | April 29 - May 2

    Anticipated re-opening is May 2. Delays may be possible the week of April 14 as road crews restore areas impacted by illegal travel and prepare for the mobile rock crusher.

    Once the six miles of road is resurfaced, individuals are urged to be respectful and Tread Lightly. Roosting around corners, driving too fast and spinning tires quickly ruins the road and the experience for others.

    For more information contact the Montpelier Ranger District at 208-847-0375. ,/p>
